About Us

Group of Seven Media Ltd. is a sales and marketing organization that represents YES TV, ATN-Asian Television Network’s media properties and numerous independent television producers. Foodies™

Foodies™ brings enjoyment and gratification to cooking! Lesia speaks to the ‘foodie’ in all of us by demonstrating how to whip up delicious meals using simple, basic, fresh ingredients. “Everyone has a little ‘foodie’ in them, and my goal is to show Canadians that cooking isn’t just for the pros.” – Lesia Burlak

Seven Days in Paradise

Wish you could languish on the white sands of your own private Caribbean beach? Now you can, as you experience the life of the rich and famous. Whether it is relaxing on the secluded island of Mustique, visiting the vacation home of Mick Jagger or partying, travel first class to the world’s most exclusive and coveted locations via Seven Days in Paradise.

Wings Over Canada

The series, now seen weekly just about everywhere in North America, is a lighthearted look at Canada, its people, history and customs. No subject escapes the crew as they work their way from ghost towns and remote fly-in fishing lakes to gold mines in search of the next unique adventure.

Canadian Rally Championships

The CRC Series covers a six-event, coast-to-coast series auto rally racing which sees the nation’s top teams competing for the championship title in the most extreme weather conditions imaginable on a challenging range of off-road courses.

Raid The North Extreme

This 6-day expedition of ‘man against nature’ race runs through British Columbia. Armed with only a map and a compass, they must select their own routes often navigating more than 500 km without course markings. Contestants have to run, mountain bike, kayak, white-water canoe and do cliff race climbing. It’s the ultimate outdoor Extreme challenge.

Targa Newfoundland

Targa Newfoundland is the first and only event of its kind to be held in North America. At 7-years young, every aspect of our high-end automotive adventure just keeps getting better. As motor sport enthusiasts, we know that limits are made to be pushed, and cars are designed to be driven.

Dave's Corner Garage

Dave brings his knowledge and love of cars to people who love cars too. With car tips, maintenance features, guest interviews and on location special segments, Dave makes it easy to love your car.

The Neighbourhood Mechanic Show

The lovable, humorous and multi-talented Dave Redinger, and his sidekick John Mitter, are the hosts of the show! The show packs 40-years of experience into a lively hour of car talk. With fun and humour, Dave and John tackle a wide range of issues critical to anyone behind the wheel. As a writer, broadcaster, certified mechanic and shop owner, Dave is uniquely qualified to answer any car related question.

The New Driver's Seat

The biggest names in the automotive scene join forces with expert automotive journalists to bring you the next generation in automotive television: The New Driver’s Seat.
